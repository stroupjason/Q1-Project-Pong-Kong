Track your ping pong scores

We did most of the heavy lifting for you. When every point counts, we want to help you record and broadcast your match scores with KongPong! Put an end to your colleague's smack talks and paddle up!

Be the Office Champion

KongPong calculates an Elo score (ranking) for each player and ranks them on the leaderboard. Visualise each player's ping pong stats for glory or shame. KongPong supports both, singles and double matches!

Slack Integration

Coming Soon! KongPong will work with Slack! Get notified when a new match is played or when the leaderboard rankings have changed, or even create a new match right from the Slack interface. Never miss another match again! You'll always in the loop as long as you're subscribed!
